Skills

Hej, I'm Kevin! An energetic student from Berlin looking for new adventures in UX!

I have lots of excitement for shaping user-centred, visually pleasing human interface interactions and have been building and designing reliable, user-centred web services for a while already.

I am used to working in digital and international surroundings, from jobs in Berlin and London. My studies taught all about software development, data bases and web services. Work as a web developer required to apply that knowledge. On the other hand I learned in order to make a product successful researching customer experiences is inevitable. Design Thinking, Prototyping and User Experience Research were key methods here. I am now a pro at working with major web technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and Bootstrap as well as tools used for mockups like Sketch. Last semester I thrived in the course 'Multimedia System Design’ where we built an app-prototype, researched and evaluated user behaviour. I am looking forward to be taking a Usability class in the coming semester. More than that am I excited to deepen my knowledge around everything UX/UI while working with you.

Working in close relation to customers and users has taught me to present my ideas openly and with confidence. I am a curious person with an eye for detail, but maybe most importantly will I add bright colours to your team!
