Project: Percy - social assistant - PWA
University: HTW Berlin
Year: 2019


A social assistant app to reduce the impact on loneliness and addictiveness of social media. In our team of six Students studying at HTW Berlin lead by David Koschnick form Berlin's Freie Universität we built a Progressive Web Application with React guided by Google Material Design.

Vision, Development and Design

social assistant as a progressive web application


  • First mock-ups / concept in InVision

  • Research other applications on the market, inspiration
  • Create, conduct, and review Google Forms online survey

  • User Research - Team internal documentation of social contacts
  • Evaluation of the results => adaptation of the concept
  • Research Design System - Google Material Design vs. Apple Human Interface Guidelines
  • Evaluation and decision for GMD with team
  • MaterialUI - React Library and GUI Framework, as a logical consequence
  • Implementation of the mocks and the design system on the real product
  • Create final mock-ups and own design system with Figma and later with the source code both based on research on usability in the ecosystem of mobile web applications
  • Compliance with the GMD guidelines
  • Implementation of the mocks and the design system on the real product
  • Creation and integration of a custom theme
  • Development of further styles and of various custom React components

  • Development of presentation mode in the application
