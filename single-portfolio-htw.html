Indoor Navigation App for HTW Berlin

from idea to research to prototype to evaluation


My aim in this course project was to conceptualize multimedia based technology that would ease the student life at HTW Berlin. Concretely the idea was to help new students to find their way across the many different buildings and rooms at the university campus using an Indoor AR navigation app. The course Multimedia System Design (Gestaltung von Multimedia-Systemen) was held by Prof. Dr. Ing. Johann Habakuk Israel.

I divided the project into the four stages:

  • Research & Focus Group
  • Paper Prototype & Heuristic Evaluation
  • Digital Prototype in Marvelapp
  • Testing & Evaluation

Each phase added new learnings leading to adaptions to the concept. E.g. a significant learning in the focus group was that an AR navigation alone may not be suitable for participants unwilling to walk around with their phone held up. While observations from the User Test were the insufficient contrast ratios of certain text in the digital prototype for instance.

Take a look at the resulting prototype here: marvelapp.com/chgfe99
