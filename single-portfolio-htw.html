UX/UI Prototype

from idea to research to prototype to evaluation


The goal of this course was to create meaningful multimedia based technology that would ease the student life at HTW Berlin as part of the course Multimedia System Design (Gestaltung von Multimedia-Systemen) held by Prof. Dr. Ing. Johann Habakuk Israel.
The project was divided into four stages: User Research & Focus Group, Paper Prototype & Heuristic Evaluation, Digital Prototype & UI, Testing & Evaluation
