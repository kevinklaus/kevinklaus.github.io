UI and Web Development for Kochhaus


The Kochhaus website is constantly changing due to my own and the team’s efforts.
Lately I have implemented advanced E-Commerce Tracking and optimised the flow and usability for new customers signing up to the subscription delivery service (Kochbox-Anmeldung).
The Voucher Landing page has previously been confusing for customers due to the high number of different options. I was able to reduce that by constraining the options to four different voucher groups to chose from leading to new individual pages.
A new internal tool at Kochhaus now allows colleagues to conveniently add complaints directly in relation to an order and it’s articles.
