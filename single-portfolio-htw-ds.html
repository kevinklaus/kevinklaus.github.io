What I built?: a Design System with Storybook
Project: Bachelor Thesis
Program: International program Media & Computing
University: HTW Berlin
Year: 2020/2021


At the beginning of the year 2020, the Covid-19 pandemic severely challenged the teaching missions of educational institutions. They were forced to close sites around the world. Hence, the demand for Distance Learning Software to help educational institutions replace teaching in presence has increased. Owing to that, the conducted work contributes a Design System realised to support the implementation of Distance Learning Software Development Projects.



Visit Design System

HTW Berlin Design System

Design System to design & develop HTW Berlin distance learning apps


Through desk research of relevant work, the needs of learners, educators, and organizations, as well as the requirements of developers and designers, were investigated. The realised Design System facilitates the collaboration between Designers and Developers, with a library of reusable UI Components as Web Componens as well as a Figma library. With the same intent, shared best practices and guidelines have been implemented. Furthermore, a study to consider the HTW Berlin Corporate Design Language has been carried out. The respectively composed User Interface Elements grant a distinctly branded User Experience applied to both Figma library as well as the Web Components. Apart from this, compliance with Accessibility guidelines was assured throughout the design of the User Interfaces. The purpose is to facilitate a reliable User Experience for learners and educators utilising applications implemented with the afforded Design System. To substantiate the work automated Accessibility testing, as well as Usability Tests, were carried out.
