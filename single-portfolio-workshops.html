Transparent Feedback Workshop

Taking the Lead on Feedback:
New Approach for Team Growth

My new approach to feedback collection and sharing that fosters a more collaborative and growth-oriented environment.

Emphasize empathy and open communication:

Moving away from the standard anonymous "Start, Stop, Continue" method. I instead create a safer and more open space for constructive criticism by holding a transparent feedback sharing workshop.

Facilitating Growth

I designed this workshop to last around 1.5 hours, it involves a cross-functional group working together virtually or in-person. Participants will prepare "Start, Stop, Continue" feedback for each other beforehand. I'll guide the session through clear steps (included visually above) to ensure a productive and respectful exchange.

Benefits of Open Dialogue

By moving beyond anonymity, we open doors to:

  • Immediate clarification: Feedback receivers can ask clarifying questions on the spot.
  • Developing honest and respectful feedback skills: We learn to communicate openly while being considerate.
  • Direct communication: We encourage direct, constructive feedback for continued improvement.
  • Growth mindset: We cultivate a culture of seeing feedback as an opportunity for individual and team growth.

The Power of Collaboration

These workshops have proven highly successful:

  • Established a safe and positive environment for peer-to-peer feedback.
  • Fostered positive reinforcement through "Continue" feedback.
  • Provided each participant with individual development opportunities.
  • Boosted employee motivation through open communication and growth opportunities.
