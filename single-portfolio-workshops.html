Transparent Feedback Workshop

Start, Stop, Continue - rethought


As quarterly performance talks are around the corner, I would like to propose a new approach of collecting and sharing feedback. This time a bit I'd like to try and avoid the standard Start, Stop, Continue feedback collected anonymously by me as the leader. In a conversation with one of my team members I had the idea to hold a transparent feedback sharing workshop.

The idea is: A cross-functional group of people that regularly collaborate meet virtually or in person for about an hour and a half. All participants note down Start, Stop, Continue feedback for all other participants prior to the meeting. We then follow the steps depicted in the graphic above (I'm super happy to explain and share this concept with you! - You need only ask).
