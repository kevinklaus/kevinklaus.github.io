Transparent Feedback Workshop

Start, Stop, Continue - rethought


As performance talks are around the corner I would like to propose an approach of collecting and sharing feedback that is a bit different from the standard anonymous Start, Stop, Continue. In a conversation with a team member came up with the idea to hold a transparent feedback sharing session between everyone rather than collecting feedback anonymously. The idea would be: We have a meeting together where all participants note down Start, Stop, Continue feedback for all other participants prior to the meeting (It's fine to skip points, if you can't think of something). Why not anonymously? The feedback doesn't just happen one dimensional and the feedback receiver can ask direct questions to feedback givers. We learn how to be honest and respectful with giving feedback. We all learn how to approach each other directly with feedback. We learn seeing feedback as a growth opportunity.
