Design Thinking and Human Values


As part of the course Design Thinking and Human Values our team of students decided to organise an Intersex Awareness Event at HTW Berlin. My responsibilities were Print Design, Social Media Campaign and Exhibition Design. Other students were in charge of a video screening and the creation of art pieces to be exhibited.
